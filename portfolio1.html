Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a simple, classic game played on a 3x3 grid by two players who take turns marking spaces with their chosen symbol, typically "X" or "O." The objective is to be the first to line up three of one's symbols in a row, column, or diagonal, which results in a win. If all nine squares are filled without any player achieving this alignment, the game ends in a draw. Known for its straightforward rules, Tic Tac Toe encourages strategic thinking as players anticipate and block each other's moves. Often used as an introduction to programming and game development, it's a fun and accessible way to learn about logic and planning.
